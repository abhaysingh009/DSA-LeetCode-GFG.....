<h2><a href="https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/addition-under-modulo/1?page=1&difficulty=Basic&status=unsolved&sortBy=submissions">Addition Under Modulo</a></h2><h3>Difficulty Level : Difficulty: Basic</h3><hr><div class="problems_problem_content__Xm_eO" style="--text-color: var(--problem-text-color);"><p><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Given three integers <strong>a</strong>, <strong>b</strong>, and <strong>M</strong>, compute the result of the <strong>modular addition</strong> operation:<br><strong><span class="katex-html" aria-hidden="true"><span class="base"><span class="mord mathnormal"><span style="font-family: math;">(</span>a</span><span class="mbin">+</span></span><span class="base"><span class="mord mathnormal">b</span><span class="mclose">) </span><span class="mbin"><span class="mord"><span class="mord mathrm">mod </span></span></span></span><span class="base"><span class="mord mathnormal">M</span></span></span></strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Note: </strong>Modular operations</span><span style="font-size: 14pt;"> returns the remainder when&nbsp;divided by M. The result will always lie in the range <strong>0</strong> and <strong>M - 1</strong>.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Examples :</strong></span></p>
<pre><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Input: </strong>a = 10, b = 20, M = 3
<strong>Output: </strong>0
<strong>Explanation: </strong>(10 + 20) mod 3 = 0</span></pre>
<pre><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Input: </strong>a = 100, b = 13, M = 107
<strong>Output: </strong>6
<strong>Explanation: </strong>(100 + 13) mod 107</span><span style="font-size: 14pt;"> = 6</span></pre>
