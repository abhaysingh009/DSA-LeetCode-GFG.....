Find the sum of the series up to <strong>nth</strong> term.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;">The Geometric Progression series looks like :- a, ar, ar<sup>2</sup>, ar<sup>3</sup>, ar<sup>4</sup>, .....&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;</span>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Examples:</strong></span></p>
<pre><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Input: </strong>n<strong> = </strong>3, a = 3, r = 2
<strong>Output: </strong>21
<strong>Explanation: </strong>Series upto 3rd term is 3 6 12, so sum will be 21.
</span></pre>
<pre><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Input: </strong>n<strong> = </strong>3, a = 1, r = 2
<strong>Output: </strong>7
<strong>Explanation</strong><strong>: </strong>Series upto 3rd term is 1 2 4, so sum will be 7.
</span></pre>
